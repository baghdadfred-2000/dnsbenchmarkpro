Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026 · Effective: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Genext Information Systems ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use DNSBenchmarkPro.com and the DNS Benchmark Pro tool (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Summary

DNS Benchmark Pro runs primarily in your browser. The DNS latency tests are performed client-side, and we do not operate accounts or databases of personal profiles. We do, however, use third-party services for geolocation and (with your consent) advertising and analytics, which may process limited data such as your IP address. The key points:

  • We request your approximate location only after you click the benchmark button, and only if you allow it. You can decline.
  • Benchmark calculations happen in your browser. We do not store your benchmark results on our servers.
  • Advertising and analytics cookies load only after you consent via our cookie banner.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location information

When you start a benchmark, your browser may ask for permission to share your location using the device Geolocation API. If you allow it, your approximate latitude/longitude is used to look up your city/region via a third-party reverse-geocoding provider (BigDataCloud). If you decline or your browser does not support geolocation, we fall back to approximate IP-based location through third-party services (ipwho.is or ipapi.co). Location is displayed to you and used to contextualize recommendations; it is not stored by us.

2.2 Technical and usage information

Like most websites, our hosting provider and the third-party services we call automatically receive your IP address, browser type, device characteristics, and timestamps as part of normal internet communication. If you consent to analytics, we may collect aggregated, non-identifying usage statistics (such as pages viewed and benchmark runs).

2.3 Cookies and similar technologies

We store your cookie-consent choice locally in your browser. With your consent, Google AdSense and analytics providers may set cookies and use similar identifiers. See our Cookie Policy for details and controls.

3. How We Use Information

  • To operate the Service and display your DNS benchmark and recommendations.
  • To approximate your location so results are relevant to your area.
  • To display advertising that helps keep the Service free (only with your consent).
  • To understand aggregate usage and improve the tool (only with your consent).
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal information. Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), California residents have the right to:

  • Delete personal information we hold about you, subject to exceptions.

  • Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. We do not sell personal information for money; however, the use of advertising cookies may be considered "sharing" under California law, which you can decline via our cookie banner.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@dnsbenchmarkpro.com. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law. If you are in the EU/UK, see Section 7.
